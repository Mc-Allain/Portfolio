BIO

Born at Muntinlupa City, Mc Allain raised by his grandparents, mother and baby-sitters. At such young age, he is already exposed to gadgets like cellphone, computer and gaming console, and because of that, he is knowledgeable enough to operate these gadgets alone compared to adults at that time, even though he is outside of the house most of the time, playing child's games.

He also plays many computer games while on Elementary and by the time when he is on 1st year high school, he took an interest in the game called Minecraft when his two close friends are talking about crafting materials. There, he showcase his skills on design especially in redstone where the player is enable to build a machine or device by the use of redstone materials which are electrical materials when compared to the real world. His friends tell him that making a device using redstone in Minecraft is not an easy task and need to take a skill or brain to build a useful one or even a random hidden technique about it. That's when he knew to himself that he has a little bit of skill when it comes to logic.

During his last years of junior high school, he took a computer specialization in TLE and learned about Computer Hardware/System Servicing (CHS/CSS). He pursues another computer specialization on senior high school and took an Information and Communications Technology strand where he outstands and outshines all of his strandmates when in comes to computer skills especially Computer Programming, and the only one who won the award of Most Outstanding Work Immersion Trainee in ICT of the batch 2018 with Best in Computer Programming and Most Innovative Capstone Project.

He is currently studying Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Polytechnic University of the Philippines, A. Mabini Campus(Sta. Mesa Branch) and still shines at Computer Programming especially in his 1st and 2nd year, before the pandemic happens. He is belong to the students who greatly affected by the new normal of educational system but still thriving to finish his run.
